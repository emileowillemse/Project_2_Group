About us

Our mission is to provide high-quality
mental healthcare and mental health
educational material accessible to all
students. We believe in providing support
help them gain the tools to work through
whatever pressures and stresses that
may come their way. We make it
accessible and frictionless to students in
order to take action and overcome
anything they might be going through.
