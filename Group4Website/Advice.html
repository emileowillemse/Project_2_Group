About Mental Health


Depression

“The silent killer” -
Depresssion has many possible causes and is usually
brought on by a variety of different factors.
Sometimes, a specific event may lead to depression.
Other times,depression seems to come on for no apparent reason,
even when life is going well.

Anxiety

Whe you have anxiety or Pain Disorder it is very important to remember that although
your feelings & symptons are very frightening, they are not dangerous or harmful.
What you are feeling is only a very strong version of the body's normal reaction to stress,
which is designed to help you and not hurt you.
